Research That Matters

Deepfake vs Real Image Detection

A research proposal for a custom lightweight CNN architecture designed to detect digital artifacts in deepfake media. Built for speed and accuracy on consumer hardware.

The Problem

Deepfake media destabilizes trust in social platforms. Existing solutions are often too heavy for real-time deployment.

The Solution

Custom CNN achieving 91% artifact detection accuracy, outperforming comparable lightweight architectures.
